Markings of a Theresian


1. She is a woman of faith and a seeker of truth with a strong sense of mission.


2. She is critically aware of the environment and of current events.


3. Her heart is open to the poor.


4. She is sensitive to the needs of persons and of community.


5. She responds creatively to the cry for justice and fullness of life.


6. She is self-directed, compassionate and life-giving in her relationships.


7. She adopts SIMPLICITY as her lifestyle.


8. She advocates PEACE as a way of life.

About the Research:

The group of researchers are composed of grade 12 students aspiring to work in a technology-inclined job in the futrure. They aim to help their school by designing a research database prototype that will help sort and organize outstanding research papers made by Theresians themselves.
